Job Specifications

At Hot Beans Web, we offer exciting opportunities in various web development roles. Below you'll find details about our current openings and the skills required for each position. We're looking for passionate individuals who are eager to learn and grow in the web development field.

Web Designer

Our Web Designers are responsible for creating visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces. They work closely with clients to understand their brand identity and translate it into beautiful, functional designs.

Key Responsibilities: Creating wireframes and mockups, designing UI elements, ensuring brand consistency, collaborating with developers, and staying updated with design trends.

Requirements: Proficiency in design tools like Adobe XD or Figma, understanding of UX principles, knowledge of HTML/CSS, and a strong portfolio.

Web Designer Image
Web Developer Image

Web Developer

Our Web Developers bring designs to life by writing clean, efficient code. They implement functionality, optimize performance, and ensure cross-browser compatibility.

Key Responsibilities: Developing responsive websites, implementing APIs, debugging and testing code, optimizing for speed and scalability, and collaborating with designers.

Requirements: Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, experience with frameworks like React or Vue, understanding of backend technologies, and problem-solving skills.
